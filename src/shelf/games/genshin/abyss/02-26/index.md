## overview

Extremely straightforward Abyss, to the point of being kind of boring for me and probably pretty annoying for anyone who doesn't have at least one Nod-Krai carry. 

It's funny (and convenient) when the characters you genuinely like also happen to be at the top of the meta. I have a lot of fun playing Flins and Zibai, and I suspect I'll be playing them a lot for a long time.
